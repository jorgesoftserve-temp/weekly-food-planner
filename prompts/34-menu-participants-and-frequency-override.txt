[Phase 2 of the MVP 1.5 plan]

Per-menu meal-frequency override + menu participants (subset of the household
this menu is for). Decisions locked in earlier in the conversation:

- Frequency override: per-member full editor at generation time. Effective
  overrides are snapshotted into `menus.generation_options.memberFrequencyOverrides`.
- Menu participants: a subset of `workspace_members` this menu is for.
  Defaults to "every active member" when the user doesn't customize.
- Folded into the same "Generate menu" form because both questions are
  "who is this menu for and how often do they eat?".
- DB representation: new `menu_participants(menu_id, member_id)` junction
  table — structural fact, not an audit blob in generation_options.
- Engine: GenerateMenuInput.members is already filtered to participants
  before the engine sees it; non-participants get no slots, period.
  Frequency cascade gains a new top step: override > member > workspace.
- Clone copies participants from source.
- Phase 4 (servings scaling) will use COUNT(menu_participants) as the
  shared-list eaters denominator.

Out of scope for this phase: servings-aware grocery scaling (Phase 4),
shop-for-subset filter (Phase 5), add-slot operation (Phase 3).
